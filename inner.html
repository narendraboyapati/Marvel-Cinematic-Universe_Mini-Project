What is "Excelsior" ?

“Excelsior” has long been Lee's catchphrase. In the mid-1960s, not long after Atlas Comics rebranded as Marvel, Lee wrote a monthly column for the comics publisher in which he'd sign off with “Excelsior!”—a Latin word meaning “ever upward.” He told io9 in 2007 that he wanted a unique word to himself that his rivals at the time wouldn't be tempted to copy.

Lee, a singular figure in American entertainment who was responsible for much of the Marvel superhero universe we know today, has demonstrated the meaning of “Excelsior!” throughout his life. He was renowned for his relentless imagination, pioneering spirit, and a heroic work ethic. The writer had worked well into his 90s, and in the last decade alone, he started an imprint for kids, published a graphic novel, and was reportedly writing a superhero rock opera.
